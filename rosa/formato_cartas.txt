POR FAVOR REVISAR ESTOS Consejos Importantes al Usar Estos Formatos
Rellenar con Sinceridad: Sustituir las frases entre corchetes ([ ]) y a añadir detalles específicos y personales sobre la relación y el carácter del solicitante. Cuanto más personales, mejor.
Estado del Firmante: Los firmantes deben ser Ciudadanos de EE. UU. (U.S. Citizen) o Residentes Permanentes Legales (Lawful Permanent Resident - LPR). Esto es crucial para la credibilidad en solicitudes de waiver.
Originalidad: Idealmente, cada carta debería ser ligeramente diferente en su contenido. Eviten usar el mismo formato exacto para todas las cartas.
Son ejemplos a usar puedes tomar el que mas aplique y modificar o usar chat gpt o Gemini o grok es major Gemini para este tipo de cartas. Pero es a consideracion. Por favor NO USAR EXACTAMENTE IGUAL por que todos tendran los mismos ejemplos
Recomendación final : Al usar los formatos, asegúrate de que cada uno añada 1-2 párrafos únicos y específicos que narren una anécdota o un detalle concreto sobre el solicitante. Ejemplo de anécdota: "Cuando tuve mi cirugía en 2023, [Alex…..] cuidó de mis hijos por tres semanas sin pedir nada a cambio, demostrando su profunda bondad y sentido de responsabilidad."
Favor de agregar copia de residencia, ciudadanía de E.U. o licencia de conducir de cada persona que proporcione una carta
Imprimir firmar y hacérmela llegar gracias de antemano por todo 
ESTE ES MI NOMBRE….       IMPORTANTE …. ALEX GIOVANNI ESPINOSA MORALES
Formato Específico de Hermano/a (Enfatizando el Lazo Fraternal y Apoyo)
Este formato es ideal para un hermano o hermana que desea resaltar la profunda conexión familiar y cómo el solicitante es una figura de apoyo constante en la vida del referente.
[Date]
U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS)
**Subject: Letter of Support for [Applicant's Full Name] (Brother/Sister)**
Dear Adjudicating Officer,
My name is [Referent's Full Name], and I am writing to strongly support the waiver application of my brother/sister, [Applicant's Full Name]. I am a **[U.S. Citizen / Lawful Permanent Resident]** of the United States.
[Applicant's First Name] is not just my sibling; they are an essential part of my life and the backbone of our extended family in the United States. We have shared [Number] years of life together, and I can attest to their exceptional moral character, dedication, and integrity.
In particular, [Applicant's First Name] provides me with crucial [mention specific type of support, e.g., emotional support during difficult times, assistance with our elderly parents, guidance for my children]. Their presence here offers our entire family a sense of stability and connection that would be impossible to replace.
I understand the seriousness of this application and want to emphasize that [Applicant's First Name] has consistently been a law-abiding and contributing member of the community. A favorable decision on their waiver would not only relieve the extreme worry and emotional distress of their immediate family but also allow them to continue enriching the lives of their siblings and relatives here.
Thank you for reviewing my testimony and considering the impact [Applicant's First Name] has on my life and the lives of those around us.
Sincerely,
[Signature]
**[Referent's Full Name]**
**Relationship to Applicant:** Brother / Sister
**U.S. Status:** U.S. Citizen / Lawful Permanent Resident (LPR)
**Phone Number:** [Referent's Phone Number]
**Email Address:** [Referent's Email Address]
**Residential Address:** [Referent's Full Residential Address]
Formato de Amigo (Enfatizando la Integración Comunitario)
Este formato es útil para mostrar la larga duración de la relación y la integración del solicitante en la vida estadounidense.

My name is [Referent's Full Name], and I am a [U.S. Citizen / Lawful Permanent Resident] of the United States. I have been friends with [Applicant's Full Name] for approximately [Number] years, since [When you met, e.g., we met at our children’s school, we worked together, we met through our church].
Throughout our long friendship, I have always known [Applicant's Full Name] to be an **outstanding and responsible person**. They consistently demonstrate [mention positive actions, e.g., respect for the law, a strong work ethic, a commitment to civic duties]. They are a well-respected member of our local [mention community group, e.g., neighborhood association, church congregation, sports team].

I respectfully request that you look favorably upon their application for a waiver.
Sincerely,

Formato de Carta de Apoyo/Patrocinio (Sponsor)
Este formato está diseñado para un amigo o familiar (ciudadano/residente) que desea formalizar su apoyo moral, social y potencialmente económico al solicitante una vez que obtenga el perdón.
[Date]
U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS)
[Address of USCIS Office – *Optional*]
**Subject: Letter of Intent to Support and Sponsor [Applicant's Full Name]**
Dear Sir/Madam,
I am writing this letter to confirm my role as a moral and social sponsor for [Applicant's Full Name] in connection with their application for a waiver of unlawful presence. I am **[Referent's Full Name]**, a **[U.S. Citizen / Lawful Permanent Resident (LPR)]** residing in the United States.
I have known [Applicant's Full Name] for [Number] years as a [Relationship to Applicant, e.g., family friend, cousin, long-time acquaintance]. I attest that they are a person of high integrity, diligence, and strong moral character.
**My commitment to [Applicant's First Name] is as follows:**
1.  **Social and Emotional Support:** I am committed to assisting [Applicant's First Name] as they proceed through the legal immigration process. I will provide ongoing encouragement and moral support to them and their family.
2.  **Community Integration:** I will actively help [Applicant's First Name] with their continued integration into the community, including assisting with [mention specific aid, e.g., finding resources for education, accessing community services, networking for employment].
3.  **Financial Intention (Optional):** While I understand that this letter does not replace the official affidavit of support (Form I-864), I confirm my personal willingness and ability to provide temporary financial assistance, if necessary, to ensure [Applicant's First Name] and their family remain stable and self-sufficient upon the approval of the waiver.
I firmly believe that [Applicant's First Name] deserves this opportunity to formalize their status and continue their positive contributions to the United States. I urge you to approve their waiver request.
Sincerely,
[Signature]
**[Referent's Full Name]**
**Relationship to Applicant:** [Specify Relationship, e.g., Family Friend, Cousin]
**U.S. Status:** U.S. Citizen / Lawful Permanent Resident (LPR)
**Occupation:** [Referent's Occupation, e.g., Teacher, Engineer, Retired]
**Phone Number:** [Referent's Phone Number]
**Email Address:** [Referent's Email Address]
**Residential Address:** [Referent's Full Residential Address]
